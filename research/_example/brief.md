# The Current State and Future of Nuclear Fusion Energy

## Research Question

What is the current state of nuclear fusion energy research and development, and what are the realistic prospects for commercial fusion power within the next two decades?

## Scope

**In scope:**
- Major fusion approaches (tokamak, stellarator, inertial confinement, emerging concepts)
- Key milestones achieved (NIF ignition, ITER progress, private sector breakthroughs)
- Private fusion companies and their approaches
- Technical challenges remaining
- Economic viability and timeline estimates
- Policy and regulatory landscape

**Out of scope:**
- Detailed plasma physics theory
- Fission energy comparisons (except where relevant to fusion context)
- Nuclear weapons applications

## Key Areas to Investigate

1. Current state of major public fusion projects (ITER, NIF, EAST, JT-60SA)
2. Private fusion sector landscape and funding
3. Key technical milestones and remaining challenges
4. Tokamak vs. stellarator vs. alternative approaches
5. High-temperature superconducting magnets and their impact
6. Economic projections and levelized cost estimates
7. Regulatory frameworks being developed for fusion

## Constraints

- Focus on developments from 2020 onward
- Prioritize verifiable data over speculation
- Standard depth research
